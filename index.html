Hello!

I am a computer scientist at Drexel University, where I also did my PhD in the field of programming language and verification, advised by Drexel UniversityColin Gordon.

Before Drexel, I was a Senior Software Engineer at CryTek. Before Crytek, I finished my M.S. courses at TU Munich and graduated with an M.S. degree from Koc University in 2015. Thanks to Microsoft Research for supporting me through a Microsoft Research scholarship during my M.S. degree studies

My work is on understanding software systems, so far mostly low-level ones, through mathematical proof. On this mission, I am interested in building program logics, the ergonomics of specifications in these logics, and, most recently, articulation (drawing them!) of proofs and specifications for programs.

My Ph.D. work was funded by my advisor's NSF Career grant.
